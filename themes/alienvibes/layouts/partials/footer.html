Area 51 Paintball

We are one of the most respected paintball fields in the Midwest. We have been serving the area for over 20 years and have a huge following. Our paintball fields are one of the most challenging in the area and our paintball players are some of the most loyal and friendly.

Our Promise

Area 51 Paintball is Northern Michigan's premier outdoor paintball field, offering great paintballing to both first time players and veterans, and is proud to have players of all ages and skill levels. Area 51 Paintball is designed to be a fun and inclusive environment for all ages and skill levels. We offer a variety of game types for both first time and experienced players, with a rotating schedule of events that keep things fresh and exciting. Whether you're a veteran of the game or a first timer, we will make your experience with us unforgettable!
